About Me

My name is Isabelle. After high school, I'd like to go to college to become a medical doctor. I know that this doesn't seem like it has much to do with programming, but really, they both come down to know how things work, and using that knowledge for constructive purposes.

From this class, I'd like to learn how to hold my own in a world that seems to get more and more technology centered by the day. Even basic coding is a highly useful skillset when it comes to the professional world.

I'd also say that I do have a background that predisposes me for coding. My father is in the field of app programming, and I tend to think in the logical, analytical manner which is required for coding, so I guess you could say that it runs in the blood.

Initially, I was apprehensive about deciding to take this class, as it was something that I've never done before. However, I hope that I learn a lot from this class, both about website design specifically, and the field of computer sciences in general.
